About Me

I am currently an MPhil student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), working under the supervision of Prof. Zhang Jize. My research focuses on leveraging Foundation Vision Models for infrastructure defect detection — building intelligent systems that can automatically identify structural flaws in critical infrastructure like bridges, roads, and buildings.

Prior to my MPhil, I earned a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics with an extended major in Artificial Intelligence from HKUST. My undergraduate research trajectory includes:

  • Establishing foundational machine learning research under the supervision of Prof. Di Shimin.
  • Advancing into medical artificial intelligence applications under the guidance of Prof. Li Xiaomeng.

Research Interests

Foundation Vision Models
